Ferienpass - Solution for Summer Course Registration

Ferienpass is a modern web application/app to replace outdated, paper-based systems for enrolling children in summer activities. Families can browse courses and register online, eliminating the need for bulky catalogs and in-person sign-ups at the local council office. Furthermore, it replaces the first-come, first-served principle with an algorithm to ensure fair course allocation.

Fair Allocation: A randomized algorithm ensures all children have equal chances to join popular courses.

Priority Lists: Parents can rank their child's preferred courses for personalized matching.

Sustainability: Fully digital, reducing paper waste.

Ferienpass makes summer planning easier, greener, and fairer for families and communities alike.

Contributors: This project is developed by an agile team of 5 software developers and designers using the Scrum methodology.
